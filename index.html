leonel andres messi born 24 June 1987 is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Spanish club Barcelona and the Argentina national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time

Messi has won a record six Ballon d'Or awards and a record six European Golden Shoes.
